About Project Phantasia

Project Phantasia represents a collaborative effort to push the boundaries of electronic and orchestral music. This project began as an experimental concept and evolved into a full-length album that explores various sonic landscapes.

The album draws inspiration from both classical composition techniques and modern electronic production, creating a unique fusion that defies traditional genre classifications.

Each track was carefully crafted to take listeners on a journey through different emotional states and imaginary worlds, making full use of dynamic range and spatial audio techniques.
