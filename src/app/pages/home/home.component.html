About Prismatic Collections

Prismatic Collections is a platform showcasing innovative musical experiences and projects, for now featuring Project Phantasia. We curate and develop interactive environments that transform how you experience music.

Each project in our collection offers unique ways to engage with music through visual art, Live2D integrations, 3D environments, and original compositions.
